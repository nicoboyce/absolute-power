Battery Chemistry Explained

LiFePO4 (Lithium Iron Phosphate) - Safer, longer-lasting, better temperature tolerance. Ideal for regular use and long-term storage.

Li-ion (Lithium-ion) - Higher energy density, lighter weight. Good for occasional use and portability.

‚ö°

Capacity Sizing Guide

Under 500Wh: Phones, tablets, small devices (1-3 days)

500-1000Wh: Laptops, small appliances, CPAP machines

1000-2000Wh: Fridges, power tools, home backup (1-2 days)

Over 2000Wh: Whole home backup, off-grid living, workshops

UPS vs Power Station

UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply): Instant switchover, computer protection, typically smaller capacity.

Power Station: Portable, higher capacity, multiple outlet types, solar charging capability.

Solar Charging

Most modern power stations support solar input. Look for MPPT controllers and compatible wattage. Popular solar panel sizes: 100W, 200W, 400W.
